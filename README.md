## üß† Major Insights

### 1. **Age Group Patterns**
- Highest survival observed in **age groups 61‚Äì70 and 51‚Äì60**.
- Survival significantly decreases among patients **above 70**.

### 2. **Biomarkers Impact**
- **Serum Creatinine** levels: Higher levels correlate with **lower survival**.
- **Ejection Fraction**: Higher ejection fraction values are associated with **better survival chances**.

### 3. **Lifestyle & Medical Risk Factors**
Risk factors evaluated across age groups include:
- Smoking
- High Blood Pressure (Hypertension)
- Diabetes
- Anemia

Older groups tend to show **multiple overlapping risk factors**, increasing fatality likelihood.
